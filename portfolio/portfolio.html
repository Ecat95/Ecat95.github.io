Modern cat art

Fall 2025

Digital

The work was inspired by an Artist Francisco at the Kaleid Gallery who did a work called pink cat. The works use of curves and color interested and inspired me. I put the color on the cat as a reference to my inspirations title.

The work uses blender to create a cat emerging from a picture frame also inspired by the subject and curved canvas of his artwork. The work uses a cat model from Cgtrader to start with and morph into the parts needed. The shadow and lighting of the work is played with letting the viewer see the 3d part of the work because of it.

The color of pink is a major point in the work with the soft creamy tone helping connect the background inside of the art frame with the subject. The color also helps contrast with the darker shadows and the golden frame the cat is coming from.
